Orange & Bergamot Signature Candle

Spark an energizing glow with our Orange & Bergamot Signature Candle. Perfect for mornings or whenever you need a boost, this single wick candle fills the air with a sunlit fragrance that sparkles with lively orange, mandarin, and blossoming neroli oil.

The Fragrance Experience

This modern classic invites you to a courtyard of orange trees in dappled shade, where lively citrus airs dance the flamenco at dawn under azure blue skies. The top notes of orange, bergamot, and mandarin awaken your senses, leading to a heart of neroli, cardamom, and galbanum that adds a unique depth. Finally, the base notes of musk, ylang-ylang, and cedarwood ground the fragrance, creating an unmistakable and radiant scent experience.

Meet The Perfumer

Beverley Bayne, Master Perfumer

Hugely fascinated by nature, Beverley draws much of her creative inspiration from the colors, light, and aromas of the landscape around her. She perceives perfumery as an emotional process, with each fragrance reflecting a little part of herself. 

Did You Know?

A vibrant metropolis where old meets new, Seville inspires our cult icon. This Andalusian suntrap is home to the most bitter orange trees in the world—14,000 of which perfume its streets or ‘calles’.
