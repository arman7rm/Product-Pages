Meet The Perfumer

Vanessa Prudent, Senior Perfumer

From her early childhood experiences in the French Normandy countryside, Vanessa Prudent has cultivated a profound passion for natural scents. Her love for nature, culinary delights, and travel infuses her fragrances with a diverse and sensitive touch, making her creations truly unique.
