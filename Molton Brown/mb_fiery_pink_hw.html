Meet The Perfumer

Carla Chabert, Senior Perfumer

The daughter of world-renowned Master Perfumer Jacques Chabert, Carla has perfume woven into her DNA. With a flair for creating unique fragrances, Carla draws inspiration from colors and bold contrasts. The Fiery Pink Pepper fragrance, a playful counterpart to her father’s iconic Re-charge Black Pepper scent, captures her creative genius and love of aromatic adventure.
