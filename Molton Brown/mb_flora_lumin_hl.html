Meet The Perfumer

Maïa Lernout, Senior Perfumer

Maïa Lernout is a Senior Perfumer with a graceful and meticulous eye, ardently passionate about expressing joy through bright floral fragrances and natural ingredients. Her elegant and modern compositions seek the light in every scent. Flora Luminare pays homage to the Tiare flower of Tahiti, known for its rare, radiant oil.

Did You Know?

One of the world's most treasured flowers, Tahitian tiare is celebrated for its delicate yet captivating scent. This flower’s sunny, blissful essence extends its radiant qualities to enhance every note, creating a unique and uplifting experience.
