Floral, Green

Fresh leafy notes accent the floral accord of spring flowers, lead by hyacinth and bluebell.

Spiced with a touch of carnation at the heart, heliotrope and ylang-ylang sustain the floral theme which is underscored by a soft balsamic and woody base in this cool green floral fragrance.

Housed in a sleek glass vessel and includes six reed sticks to intensify the signature aroma.
