Did You Know?

Inspired by Master Perfumer Jacques Chabert’s visit to a tropical botanical garden, this fragrance captures the heady scent of an exceptional type of ginger flower. By blending it with aquatic notes, the fragrance is a modern twist on the iconic ’90s marine perfume trend.
