Meet The Perfumer

Carla Chabert, Senior Perfumer

As the daughter of Jacques Chabert, world-renowned Master Perfumer and creator of our most iconic scents, Carla has fragrance woven into her very DNA. Inspired by colour and punctuated with playful, deliberate flaws, her creations are far from ordinary.

Did You Know?

This unique fragrance was inspired by a trip to a tropical botanical garden, where Master Perfumer Jacques Chabert discovered an exotic ginger flower. This rare bloom adds a floral twist to the classic woody and spicy notes, resulting in a fresh, modern interpretation of the ‘90s marine perfumery trend.
