The Fragrance Story

Set sail on a fragrant adventure. Crisp fig leaves and vibrant cardamom dance with joyful bergamot, while earthy cypress and cedarwood bring balance, capturing the untamed beauty of the sea. This lotion is perfect for those who crave exploration and love the scent of the ocean breeze.

Coastal. Bold. Intrepid.

Meet The Perfumer

Carla Chabert, Senior Perfumer

Crafted by Carla Chabert, daughter of Master Perfumer Jacques Chabert, this hand lotion represents her creative and playful approach to fragrance, inspired by her love for color and unconventional beauty.

Did You Know?

Blue cypress, sourced from Northern Australia, is the world's only naturally blue essential oil. Its refreshing, steam-distilled essence enhances the lotion’s invigorating marine notes.
