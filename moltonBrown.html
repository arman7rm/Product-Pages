Did You Know?

Blue cypress is a rare and fascinating essential oil, renowned for its natural blue hue. Sourced from the cypress conifers of Northern Australia, this essential oil is steam-distilled, creating a vibrant, refreshing scent that enhances the gel's marine appeal.
