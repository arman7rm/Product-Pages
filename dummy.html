The Fragrance Story

Imagine a crimson summer tart sitting on the pantry table, alongside freshly picked roses, scattered pastry crumbs, and glossy ribbons of rhubarb. The Delicious Rhubarb & Rose Fine Liquid Hand Wash is a deliciously sweet treat for your senses, adding a citrus twist to your daily hand care routine.

Vivacious. Fizzy. Tempting.

Meet The Perfumer

Vanessa Prudent, Senior Perfumer

Vanessa Prudent’s love of natural scents began during childhood, as she explored the French Normandy countryside on horseback. Her passion for nature, food, and travel brings a unique sensitivity to her fragrances. Delicious Rhubarb & Rose captures Vanessa’s vibrant and joyful approach to perfumery, infusing every drop with her love of fruity, floral scents.

Why You’ll Love It

This vegan hand wash not only cleanses but elevates your senses with its exquisite fragrance. Fresh rhubarb and sparkling citrus create a fizzy, fruity burst of energy, balanced by a base of creamy vanilla for a well-rounded scent experience.
