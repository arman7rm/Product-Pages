Grether’s Pastilles is the best vocal care remedy for the throat and voice. Handcrafted in Switzerland over 150 years ago, our traditional pastilles have been recognized around the world to protect the vocal cords, soothe the throat, combat dry mouth and help with hoarse voice. If you are a performer, present in public or rely on your voice for your livelihood, Grether’s Pastilles should be part of your routine.

Grether’s Pastilles are a unique blend of powerful ingredients formulated to provide effective relief from irritated throats caused by excessive taking, singing, or speaking. Its intense flavors provide a refreshing taste, while the glycerin helps moisten and soothe the irritated mucous membranes in the mouth and throat.

To consume

Dissolve one pastille in the mouth to allow the glycerin to coat the throat. Repeat every two hours, if required. Do not consume in excess, as the pastilles may have a laxative effect.

Safety Information

The sugarfree varieties of Grether’s Pastilles contain maltitol that may have a laxative effect when consumed in excess.
